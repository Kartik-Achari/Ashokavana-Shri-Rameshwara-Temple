ABOUT


The precise location of Ashokavana Shri Rameshwara Temple is Dodamangadi Tree Park (Mullur Gudda), which stands 2.3km from Ramdurg, Belagavi district of Karnataka state, India.




About the Shiva Murti



To honour Lord Shiva, the Karnataka Government commissioned a Shiva Murti on the outskirts of Ramdurg, a town in the state’s Belgaum district.


• The statue was unveiled in 2018, and draws hundreds of thousands of devotees, tourists, and visitors to date.

• It was raised to commemorate the Mahashivaratri festival that year.

• At 78 feet tall, this is one of India’s tallest Shiva Murti statues.

• Ramdurg’s Shiva Murti cost the state around INR 1.5 Crore to construct.

• Working on the statue’s every nuance and detail were Sridhar Murthy and his family, skilled artisans all.


The regional MLA of Ramdurg, Ashok Pattan, performed the grand unveiling in the company of hundreds of devotees and local leaders. Ashok spearheaded the campaign for the statue. He went on record to state, “I took up this project as per the will of my parents…Ramdurg a tourist destination. Many joined hands in completion...I thank them all.”




About the Nandi Idol



• At Ashokavan of Rameshwar temple in Ramadurga taluk, the result of the efforts of MLA Ashoka Pattan.
• The hill is further made attractive by installing a Nandi (Basavanna) idol in front.
• Sculptors have worked for two years to prepare this idol which is 22 feet high, 32 feet long and 14 feet wide.
• It is the tallest Nandi idol in the country.




About The Salumarada Thimmakka Vrukshodhyana


Apart from The Magnificent statue of Lord shiva and Nandi, we can also witness the beautiful scenic view from top of the hill, and a quiet relaxing park while entering the premises named Salumarada Thimmakka Vrukshodhyana Dodamangadi.

• This park is dedicated to Thimmakka also known as Aala Marada Thimmakka who is an Indian environmentalist from Karnataka state, noted for her work in planting and tending to 385 banyan trees along a 45 Kilometres stretch of road from Hulikal and Kudur. She has also planted 8000 other trees with support of her husband. She is also a Padma Shri and National Citizenship Awardee.
• This park is full of greenery and consist of playables such as swings, seesaw, slides etc for the kids. Here and there we can also find the benches for sitting and enjoying the beautiful nature.
